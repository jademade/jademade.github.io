works

"less is more"
a hollographic pyramid

strategy, concept, content, design

"Less is more" is a communication strategy designed to revitalize the German Pavilion, designed by Mies van der Rohe for the 1929 International Exhibition in Barcelona, which today goes virtually unnoticed.

Based on a thorough analysis of the building and its history, we created a campaign centered around a portable pyramid structure, capable of projecting holographic videos from any smart phone. It is a story from a bygone era told to new generations through today's lens.
